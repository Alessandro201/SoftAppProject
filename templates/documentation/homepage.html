Project Overview

We have designed a website to work with two datasets .
The website is composed by three parts which interact to analyze, convert the data in a usable format and present the results to the user. They are respectively "functions.py", "mediator.py" and "website.py".
Following the specification requested on the goal of the project, Part 2, "functions.py", is the core of the analysis and contains all the classes and methods needed in order to perform the operations on the datasets. Part3 is "website.py" and contains all the functions needed to interact with the user through a webpageand present him the results. While part 1 is "mediator.py" and is the component which links the other two parts and allows them to communicate.
